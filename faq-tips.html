FAQ & TIPS

Lets clarify some things & maybe some tips to have a nice tour with us

FAQ

Half day tours?

All tours are available on daily basis for a day or sunset tour and duration of approximately of 4,5 - 5 hours. Day tours start at 10:00 and disembarkation is at 03:00 pm, whereas Sunset tours start at 03:00 pm and finish after sunset.

Full day & overnight charters?

Full day & overnight charters are available on private basis only and upon request. Full day is considered the charter starting from 10:00 am and finishing after sunset, whereas on the overnight charter starting at 10:00 am and finishes the same time on the next day. The maximum number of guests that can be accommodated in a vessel is 4 couples or 8 guests in total per vessel.

Swimming stops, Volcano, beaches and other sights?

All our tours include a stop at the volcanic "warm springs", anchor at famous beaches and secluded private bays for swimming and snorkeling. The time allocated at each swim stop is dependent on the weather conditions and varies between 20 - 30 minutes.

What should we bring with us?

Whatever you need on a day at the beach:
- Hat
- Sun glasses
- Bathing Suit
- Sun Block
- Camera
- A light sweater for the afternoon tours.
- Towels for the Guests that join the Deluxe & Dreamcatcher tours

Are towels provided onboard?

We do provide towels on all Exclusive & Private tours, guests joining the Deluxe & Dream Catcher tour should bring their own towels.

Swimming and snorkeling equipment?

Snorkeling equipment (Mask and Snorkel) are available for all guests on board.Floating noodles are also available on all our vessels.

Are vegetarian options available?

All our meals are well balanced to include vegetarian dishes even without notifying us.

Are there Life-Vests on board?

According to Greek Maritime Law, all charter vessels are equipped with the appropriate number of Life-Vests and Life-Raft on board.

Tips for a better experience

Providing your email and a contact number is the best way to ensure you will be informed for all the details or amendments of your reservation/ pickup info / delays etc

Santorini is not famous for the best roads, so especially during pick season delays on the transfer service often occur, please be patient and contact our office number for an update!

Be extra cautious!!!! There are many companies that offer sailing tours and boat excursions in Santorini! Please reconfirm your name with the driver to assure that you are being picked up by the correct company and driver, otherwise you will be one of the cases that often loose their tour.

All check-in links visualise through google maps and provide exact location of your pickup place!
Reassure that you understand the location and you are accessible by the driver in order to avoid delays.

After disembarkation check with the stuff and crew to confirm your transfer back to Hotel because the vehicle and driver might have changed!

Respect your fellow travelers!
