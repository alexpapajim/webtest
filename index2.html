Semi Private Tours

Group tours run daily on all vessels for both day and sunset itinerary.
Each tour has its own characteristics regarding the amount of Guests that may accommodate on board, food and beverage options available and itinerary.
We are driven from the experience we want to provide on-board and the "journey" rather than the destination therefore all tours start from the south side of the island in Vlichada marina which is the safest for passenger embarkation.
