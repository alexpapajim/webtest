Triton

Triton is our prestigious power catamaran, the fastest of our vessels, and the only one of its kind for charter in Greece. It's the island's most luxurious and stable power Catamaran. Triton approaches bays and beaches that most other vessels cannot reach, offering privacy and great comfort. Triton's Cruising flexibility guarantees the most comprehensive
tour of the island with multiple stops for swimming and snorkeling.
You can sit back and enjoy the breathtaking scenery on board.

Adult (12+): 190€
Child (5 - 12): 120€
Infant (0 - 4): 15€
